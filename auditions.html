Auditions

Students wishing to audition for the Interlude Orchestra should follow the steps below.

  1. Fill out the google survey HERE
  2. Wait for audition materials to arrive via email Between March 1 st and 3rd. Some instruments require solo playing for the auditions; students may look at these requirements and begin to work on this part before the excerpts are sent via email.
  3. Make a VIDEO AUDITION and upload it to the google drive folder shared with you over email. Title your video (s) LAST NAME, INSTRUMENT, Interlude 2019. The video recording may be completed with a smartphone or other filming device with decent audio capabilities. We do not care about visual quality, although we would like to be able to see you when you are playing.
  4. Upload your video audition by APRIL 4th.
  5. Wait for an email that will list accepted members by APRIL 15th.
